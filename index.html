About Me

I’m Greg Alexander

I’ve been in the fitness industry since 2010 with over 12 years of experience coaching and learning a large array of specialised training techniques from the very best in the industry, including olympic medalist gymnasts and weight lifters.

I’m dedicated to continual learning and improving my knowledge so I can provide the very best the industry has to offer to you right here in Mildura.

I specialise in creating specific outcomes for my clients and I possess a wide range of experience and techniques to draw on to deliver the most efficient outcome for my clients. If you are after rehab, sport specific, body composition, pain resolution, just to name a few, I can offer you a unique solution to suit your circumstances.
